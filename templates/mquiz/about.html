mQuiz allows you to create online quizzes which your friends can answer on their mobile device.

You can create and edit your quiz online with our easy to use tool, or use our API.

Quiz results can be uploaded to our website and compare your scores with your friends!

You can view summary information about who has responded, average scores and more.
